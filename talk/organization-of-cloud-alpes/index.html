Organization of Cloud Alpes

David Aparicio
Website
Image credit: Simon Berger (Unsplash)

Abstract

I’m playing an active role in organising this first edition of Cloud Alpes. Cloud Alpes is a Lyon-based group whose aim is to showcase the technological excellence of France and the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, by organising an annual conference with a European dimension. This event is a high point that brings together both local players and technology leaders, enabling all participants to leave with a desire to innovate and promote IT excellence in their projects and activities.
